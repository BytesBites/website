Welcome to IQRA English Institute

Iqra English School opened in 2000 with the vision to be a school of the future, combining a traditional liberal arts program with new pedagogical and technological techniques for the 21st century.

Today, two decades after we first opened our doors, we continue to be strongly student-centered and optimistically future-oriented in our approach. Iqra English School holds dual accreditation from the Iqra foundation (IFFS) and AdvancED, and we currently enroll 771 students. We invite you to explore our website to learn more about our learning community and our efforts to nurture curiosity, inspire creativity, foster team work and fuel individual growth.
