Our Goal

IQRA FOUNDATION

Iqra school aims to provide each student an education that will enable him or her to live a moral, happy and productive life. The development of intellect is central to such a life and, thus, intellectual endeavor and growth are the primary work of the school. The acquisition of knowledge; the development of skills, critical judgment and intellectual curiosity; and increased understanding are the goals of the school's academic program.

School Values

As a responsible school, we take seriously the charge to experiment and innovate. Six times a year, Ex Days extend periods so that student can have longer classes (95 minutes instead of 45) for greater depth in laboratories, simulations, and projects. Twice a year, Flex Days give students four half days to explore curricular subjects in a field experience or in another extended and in-depth manner. Twice a year, Community Work Days offer students choices to learn how they can understand community needs and contribute locally.

Our school was founded on three important ideas: • To provide a challenging, college-preparatory education, • To encourage learning in an active, social, and creative environment, and • To nurture and sustain citizens while fostering citizenship in an academic setting

Each year our students have distinguished themselves by doing well on the state-mandated End-of-Course tests. 100 Percent results in Final year exam of 10th Standard Since 2014 - 2018
